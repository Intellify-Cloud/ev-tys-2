How affordability is worked out

Filter 1: monthly bond repayments are typically capped at 30% of gross monthly income.

Filter 2: banks also check your net surplus income after total monthly expenses are deducted from net monthly income. Current rent should generally be excluded if it stops after you move.

The lower figure wins: this maximum monthly instalment is converted into an estimated bond amount using the standard amortization formula. Banks may also stress test your budget at a higher interest rate.
