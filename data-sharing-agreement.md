## Data Sharing Agreement

### Definitions

**We, us and our:** Refers to **{{ site.company }}**, a private company with limited liability registered under the company laws of the Republic of South Africa. The principal place of business is {{ site.address }}.

**Business Partner:** Refers to any real estate agency or other business partner that shares a third party's personal information in any form with **{{ site.company }}**.

**Third Party:** Refers to any customer of a Business Partner or other data subject whose personal information is shared by the Business Partner with any member of **{{ site.company }}**.

**Third Party's personal information:** Refers to personal information about the Third Party, their spouse, or dependants, where relevant. It includes information about financial status, gender, age, contact numbers, and addresses.

**Processing of information:** Means the automated or manual activity of collecting, recording, organising, storing, updating, sharing, distributing, removing, or deleting personal information.

---

### Purpose of This Data Sharing Agreement

The purpose of this Data Sharing Agreement, as read with our Privacy Statement, is to set out how **{{ site.company }}** collects, uses, shares, and otherwise processes all Third Party personal information in line with the Protection of Personal Information Act ("POPIA"). This Data Sharing Agreement will be effective from 1 July 2021.

After contacting any Third Party, **{{ site.company }}** will only further process the Third Party's personal information in terms of a specific mandate with the Third Party, should the Third Party enter into such a mandate with **{{ site.company }}**.

Any such mandate will be subject to the **{{ site.company }}** Privacy Statement. Should the Third Party not conclude a mandate with **{{ site.company }}**, then **{{ site.company }}** will not retain any personal information of the Third Party unless required by law.

---

### Commitment to Privacy and POPIA Compliance

**{{ site.company }}** is committed to protecting all data subjects' right to privacy.

As our valued Business Partner, we want you to have the assurance that when you engage with **{{ site.company }}**, any Third Party's personal information shared with us will be treated subject to the **{{ site.company }}** Privacy Policy and the requirements of POPIA.

You should only share Third Party personal information with us if you have the Third Party's consent or are otherwise entitled to share such information by reason of a processing justification under POPIA or any other reason. You should only share information that is necessary, and you should inform the Third Party that you have shared the information with us.

Should the Third Party withdraw their consent given to you, you should notify us as soon as possible.

In your privacy notices with your customers whose information you might share with **{{ site.company }}**, you should inform them that any such sharing of personal information with **{{ site.company }}** will be subject to this Data Sharing Agreement and the **{{ site.company }}** Privacy Policy. You should specifically tell them that:

- The Third Party may withdraw their consent at any time.
- Should the Third Party withdraw such consent or not grant **{{ site.company }}** a specific mandate, then **{{ site.company }}** will not retain any personal information of the Third Party.

We will take all reasonable steps to keep all Third Party personal information safe and confidential, and will comply with any undertakings given by you to the Third Party regarding their personal information. Alternatively, we will delete their personal information from our record-keeping systems.

Should you receive any complaints from a Third Party regarding any personal information shared with us, you will notify us as soon as reasonably possible so that we can address the complaint. You can do so by emailing **{{ site.email }}** or by otherwise making contact with us.

---

### Binding Nature of This Data Sharing Agreement

Should you share a Third Party's personal information with any member of **{{ site.company }}**, then **{{ site.company }}** will comply with the terms of this Data Sharing Agreement and the **{{ site.company }}** Privacy Policy.

This undertaking is given in your favour and in favour of the Third Party. The Third Party may rely on the undertakings given by us in terms of the **{{ site.company }}** Privacy Policy.

---

### Acceptance Is Voluntary

Any Third Party has the right to object to the processing of their personal information. It is voluntary for the Third Party to accept the terms and conditions of the **{{ site.company }}** Privacy Policy. A Third Party may withdraw their consent at any time.

---

### Purpose for Processing Third Party Personal Information

We undertake in favour of our Business Partner and Third Parties that we will only process Third Party personal information for the following purposes:

1. Applying for home loan finance on behalf of the Third Party and any home loan, home-related, and personal lines insurance products.
2. Applying for any other product or service specifically requested by the Third Party or by the Business Partner.
3. Advising the Third Party about related products and services that they may qualify for, as offered by any company within **{{ site.group }}**.
4. Advising the Third Party about related products and services that they may qualify for, as offered by any third-party provider that offers related products and services.

We will ensure that any such third-party provider has agreed to keep the information confidential and that the information can only be used for a specific agreed purpose.

---

### Privacy Preferences

We will not use Third Party personal information for a different purpose than the one stated when you provided the data to us. A Third Party may opt out of communications about updated information, new services, and marketing messages at any time.

---

### Confidentiality

We will keep Third Party personal information confidential, notwithstanding the source it is collected from, whether from our Business Partner, the Third Party, or any other source.

---

### Sharing of Personal Information

If a third party asks us for any Third Party personal information, we will only share it with them if:

1. The Third Party has given consent for the disclosure of this information to that third party for the provision of services, for example financial institutions, lenders, insurance companies, deposit-taking institutions, and FICA verification agencies.
2. The person or organisation has a legal right to access the information, for example regulators and/or any party entitled to such information in terms of a contract with the Third Party.

---

### Sharing for Research and Improvement

While **{{ site.company }}** may share and combine all Third Party personal information for market, statistical, and academic research purposes, in such cases the personal information will be de-identified.

---

### Direct Electronic Marketing and Opting Out

**{{ site.company }}** will not use information provided by a Business Partner for direct electronic marketing unless it has obtained the Third Party's consent to that. Additionally, **{{ site.company }}** will always permit a Third Party to opt out of any communications from **{{ site.company }}**.

---

### Third Party Rights

All Third Parties have the right to know what personal information we hold about them and have the right to ask us to update, correct, or delete their personal information.

Where we cannot delete their personal information, we will take all steps to make it anonymous. Examples of where we may not be able to delete personal information include where the law requires us to keep it.

Examples of laws that require us to collect and keep personal information include:

- The Electronic Communications and Transactions Act (ECT)
- The Financial Intelligence Centre Act (FICA)
- The Financial Advisory and Intermediary Services Act (FAIS)
- The National Credit Act (NCA)
- The Consumer Protection Act (CPA)
- The Long-term Insurance Act (LTIA)
- The Financial Sector Regulation Act (FSRA)

---

### Transferring Personal Information Outside South Africa

We will take reasonable steps to ensure that any country, company, or person to which we pass personal information, for example for the use of cloud services, agrees or is obliged by law in that jurisdiction to treat personal information with the same level of protection as required by the laws of South Africa.

---

### Changes to This Data Sharing Agreement

We may change this Data Sharing Agreement at any time. The most updated version will always be available on {{ site.url }} and will be effective only from publication on our website.
